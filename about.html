Meet Our Team

Our team formed out of a common interest for helping organizations locate technological resources to advance the causes they stand for. On the flip side, as computer science students at Emory University, we have all experienced the difficulties many beginning programmers face. To get a job, you need experience, but it's hard to get experience without a job. We hope this platform will be able to fill the void for both of these needs, and better the world in the process.
